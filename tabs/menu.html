Coffee


Cappuccino

Espresso-based coffee that involve the use of cream instead of milk, using non-dairy milk substitutes and flavoring with cinnamon or chocolate powder

Cold coffee

Iced coffee is a coffee beverage served cold by brewing coffee then serving it over cold milk, or by brewing the coffee cold.

Black coffee

Black coffee is simply coffee that is normally brewed without the addition of sugar, milk, cream, or added flavors. While it has a slightly bitter taste compared to when it is flavored.


Sandwich


Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A grilled cheese sandwich is assembled by creating a cheese filling of cheddar, between two slices of bread,stuffing of requirement and is then heated until the bread browns.

Club Sandwich

A club sandwich is a combo toasted bread, sliced cooked Chicken(or veg stuffing), lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise. The best one for stasifying hunger.

Ice Cream Sandwich

An ice cream sandwich is a frozen dessert consisting of ice cream of flavour as per requirement between two bread,bicotte or cookies.


Pasta


Pasta

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with white or red or pink sause, and formed intomany shapes, then cooked by baking.

Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta made of milled wheat and water and enriched with vitamins and minerals.

Lasagne

Lasagne are a type of wide, flat pasta,made of stacked layers of thin flat pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù and other vegetables..

Mojito
